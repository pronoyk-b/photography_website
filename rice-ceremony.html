Celebrating Your Child's Special Day

The Rice Ceremony (Annaprashan) is a significant milestone in a child's life, marking their first solid food. This beautiful tradition deserves to be captured with care and attention to detail.

Our Rice Ceremony photography package is designed to capture all the joy, emotions, and cultural significance of this special occasion. We focus on the little details that make this day memorable - from the decorations to the expressions of joy on your child's face.
